\chapter{strings of tempest}
\label{sec:strings_tempest}
\lhead[tempest]{}
\lstset{style=6502Style}

We covered the mechanics of generating strings in \textit{Tempest} in the earlier chapter
\hyperref[sec:language]{\textcolor{blue}{'macro language fun'}}. So here we'll take a small
selection of the message strings used in \textit{Tempest} and recreate how they appear
on screen.
